# Installing RAWBY on Android / Windows / Mac (Chrome)

RAWBY is a Progressive Web App (PWA). You can install it directly from Chrome — it works like a real app, runs offline, and sits on your home screen or desktop. No app store required.

---

## On Android (Chrome)

1. Open **Chrome** on your Android device
2. Go to the RAWBY link: `https://rawby-1.onrender.com` *(or the link sent to you)*
3. Wait for the page to fully load
4. Tap the **three dots (⋮)** menu in the top right
5. Tap **"Add to Home screen"** or **"Install app"**
6. Tap **Add** on the confirmation prompt
7. RAWBY appears on your home screen — tap it to open

> If you don't see "Install app", look for a small install icon (square with arrow) in the address bar.

---

## On Windows or Mac (Chrome desktop)

1. Open **Chrome**
2. Go to the RAWBY link
3. Look for the **install icon** (square with down arrow) in the address bar on the right side
4. Click it, then click **Install**
5. RAWBY opens as a standalone window and is added to your taskbar / dock

---

## Why install instead of using the browser tab?

- Works like a native app — no browser chrome, full screen
- Faster loading (cached locally)
- Stays signed in
- Gets its own icon on your home screen / desktop

---

## Uninstalling

**Android:** Long-press the RAWBY icon → **Remove** or **Uninstall**  
**Windows:** Settings → Apps → find RAWBY → Uninstall  
**Mac:** Open RAWBY → three dots menu → Uninstall RAWBY

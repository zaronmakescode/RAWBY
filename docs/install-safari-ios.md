# Installing RAWBY on iPhone or iPad (Safari)

RAWBY is a Progressive Web App (PWA). On iOS and iPadOS, you install it through Safari — it lands on your home screen and works just like an app from the App Store.

---

## Requirements

- iPhone or iPad running **iOS / iPadOS 16.4 or newer** (recommended — best PWA support)
- **Safari** browser (Chrome on iOS cannot install PWAs)

---

## Step-by-step

1. Open **Safari** on your iPhone or iPad
2. Go to: `https://rawby-1.onrender.com` *(or the link sent to you)*
3. Wait for the page to fully load — do not skip this step
4. Tap the **Share button** at the bottom of the screen (box with an arrow pointing up)
5. Scroll down in the share sheet and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
6. The name will show as **RAWBY** — tap **Add** in the top right
7. Go to your home screen — the RAWBY icon is there

Tap the icon to open RAWBY. It opens fullscreen, without the Safari address bar.

---

## Tips

- **Stay signed in:** RAWBY saves your session. As long as you open it from the home screen icon (not Safari directly), you stay logged in.
- **Allow notifications:** When prompted, tap Allow to get weekly challenge reminders.
- **iPadOS:** The same steps apply. The Share button is in the toolbar at the top right.

---

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Fix |
|---------|-----|
| No "Add to Home Screen" option | Make sure you're using **Safari**, not Chrome or Firefox |
| App asks you to log in every time | Open from the home screen icon, not from Safari |
| Blank screen on open | Close and reopen — first load may take a few seconds |
| Page won't load | Check your internet connection, then try again |

---

## Note on iOS PWA limitations

Apple's PWA support is solid on iOS 16.4+, but a few things differ from App Store apps:
- Push notifications require iOS 16.4+
- Camera access works normally in Safari
- No App Store listing — you share the link directly
